Get Portland Baked!

There’s an organic aroma wafting through Portland, and it all emanates from 340 NE 11th. From bike rides to Benson bubblers, craft breweries to chicken coops, we’re fueling your Portland lifestyle with organic breads baked in the city, for the city. Franz Bakery has been Portland’s bakery for 110 years, and as the city has evolved into a hub of organic bliss, we’ve evolved right along with it. Today our wholesome USDA-certified organic breads are as rich with flavor and texture as the city itself. So grab your crew: the tattooed hikers and naked bikers, the voracious foodies and cool coasties, the bearded brewers and green growers. It’s time to get Portland baked.
